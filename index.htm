About the Game

Harsh Habitats is a game of survival and civilization building that whisks one off to a planet where your decisions are shaped by both the most hostile forces of nature and the ancient challenges of the time. Set against the tapestry of prehistoric times, you take command of a fledgling tribe, heading them through awful environments, gathering resources, and building the foundation for a successful society. Fight savage beasts, unpredictable weather conditions, and fight for survival as you spearhead your people through the ages from humble beginnings to a powerful civilization. Help your people unlock secrets of the past by using strategic planning, resource management, and a pinch of creativity to set up a legacy in this wild land.

Key Features

  • Build A Civilization: Lay the foundations of your empire, gather resources, and grow your influence.
  • Attack Others: Bring out your fleet and destroy!
  • Rule the World: Dominate the land, expand your reach, and secure your place.
