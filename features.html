Features of Harsh Habitats

Harsh Habitats is a thrilling game of survival and civilization building that whisks players away to an alien planet where your decisions are influenced by both the harsh forces of nature and the challenges of ancient times. Set against the backdrop of a prehistoric world, you take command of a fledgling tribe, leading them through treacherous environments, gathering essential resources, and establishing the foundation for a thriving society.

As you navigate the unforgiving landscape, you will face savage beasts and unpredictable weather conditions, all while fighting for your people's survival. Your journey will take you from humble beginnings to the emergence of a powerful civilization. Your decisions will be crucial as you help your people unlock the secrets of the past, employing strategic planning, resource management, and a touch of creativity to establish a lasting legacy in this wild land.

Key Features

Build A Civilization

Lay the foundations of your empire by gathering resources and expanding your influence. Develop your tribe into a thriving civilization, ensuring the survival of your people while competing against rivals.

Attack Others

Mobilize your fleet to conquer your enemies! Engage in strategic warfare and make tactical decisions to outmaneuver opponents, using your resources wisely to bolster your strength.

Rule the World

Dominate the land, expand your reach, and secure your place as a powerful leader. With each conquest, you’ll shape the future of your civilization and carve out your legacy in history.

Join the journey in Harsh Habitats, where every decision matters and every battle counts. Embrace the challenge of survival, and rise from the ashes of adversity to build an empire that stands the test of time.
