About Harsh Habitats

Harsh Habitats is a 3-D civilization-building and survival game developed by Xelionix GP. It features mild violence, no blood or gore, comic mischief, and very mild off-screen suggestive activity, making it suitable for an E10+ audience.

Features

  • Build and manage your own civilization
  • Survive in harsh environments
  • Engage in strategic gameplay
  • Explore diverse biomes
  • Experience rich, immersive graphics
